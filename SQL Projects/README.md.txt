#  Online Food Delivery Analytics - Real World SQL Project

This project simulates a real-world online food delivery system (like Zomato/Swiggy). It involves customers placing orders from restaurants, and the data is analyzed to extract business insights.

## Schema Includes:
- Customers: Name and city of users
- Restaurants: Restaurant name, city, and food category
- Orders: Order amount, delivery partner, and order details

##  Key Business Questions Answered:
- Who are the top-spending customers?
- Which cities generate the most orders?
- Which food category brings in the most revenue?
- Which delivery partner is most used?

##  Skills Used:
- SQL Joins
- Aggregations
- GROUP BY, ORDER BY
- Real-world business logic and KPIs

##  Why This Project?
This project reflects real business cases for food delivery companies, helping data analysts:
- Monitor sales performance
- Evaluate customer value
- Optimize delivery logistics
- Support data-driven decision making.
